Who I Am

I'm a Senior Android Developer based in Tachileik, Myanmar, with over 5 years of professional experience building production-grade mobile applications.

My core expertise lies in Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Firebase, and architecting scalable systems with MVVM & Clean Architecture. I also develop web experiences using Three.js and integrate cutting-edge AI through the Gemini API.

Beyond coding, I'm deeply passionate about developer education — creating content specifically tailored for Burmese-speaking developers, helping bridge the gap in local tech communities.
